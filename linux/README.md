### Load system
- systemctl `/etc/systemd/system/.` -> `/usr/lib/systemd/system/.`
	- enable | disable
	- start | stop
	- reload | restart
	- mask | unmask
- openrc

Run level | Systemd | SysVinit
--- | --- | ---
0 | power-off | halt
1 | rescue | single-user
2 | multi-user | multi-user - NFS
3 | multi-user | multi-user + NFS + Net
4 | multi-user | custom
5 | graphical | multi-user + NFS + Net + GUI
6 | reboot | reboot
